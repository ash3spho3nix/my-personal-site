## 🧠 Deep Learning

- [The Deep Learning Book (Goodfellow et al.)](https://goodfeli.github.io/dlbook/) — The canonical reference
- [Neural Networks and Deep Learning](http://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com/) — Michael Nielsen's accessible intro
- [Deep Learning Online Courses & Materials](https://aindrasystems.wordpress.com/2015/04/11/deep-learning-online-courses-reading-materials-and-software-packages/)
- [MIT Press Free Deep Learning Book](http://www.analyticbridge.com/group/books/forum/topics/free-deep-learning-book-mit-press)
- [UB CSE 705 — ML course](http://www.cse.buffalo.edu/~hungngo/classes/2015/705/)
- [ArXiv: Deep Learning review (2013)](http://arxiv.org/abs/1305.0445)

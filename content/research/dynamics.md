## ⚙️ System Dynamics  

Understanding how systems evolve over time is central to most engineering problems.  

This includes:
- transient behavior  
- nonlinear response  
- stability and instability  

---

## 🔍 Focus  

Interest in:
- multi-body systems  
- coupled dynamics  
- energy transfer mechanisms  

---

## 🧠 Perspective  

Rather than only solving equations, the goal is to understand:
> how behavior emerges from system interactions

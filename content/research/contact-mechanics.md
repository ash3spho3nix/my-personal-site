## ⚙️ Contact Mechanics  

Contact interactions are inherently nonlinear and complex.  

They influence:
- friction  
- wear  
- stress distribution  

---

## 🔬 Applications  

- tire-road interaction  
- piston ring behavior  
- mechanical interfaces  

---

## 🧠 Perspective  

Contact problems often reveal:
> how local interactions influence global system behavior

## 🌱 Evolution of Systems  

Systems rarely remain static — they evolve.  

This applies to:
- mechanical systems  
- energy systems  
- even models themselves  

---

## 🔬 Focus Areas  

- parameter evolution  
- degradation behavior  
- long-term system response  

---

## 🧠 Insight  

Understanding evolution helps in:
- predicting failure  
- designing robust systems  
- improving models over time

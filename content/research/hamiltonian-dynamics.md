## 🔄 Hamiltonian Systems  

A framework to describe systems in terms of energy rather than forces.  

---

## ⚡ Why It Matters  

Provides:
- deeper insight into system structure  
- conservation laws  
- long-term behavior  

---

## 🧠 Interest  

Using such formulations to:
- understand system stability  
- analyze complex dynamics  
- connect mathematical structure to physical behavior
